About SAC

We are a student-run council at Turner Fenton Secondary School! SAC acts as the voice of both the students and the staff; bringing opinions and fresh new ideas to the table while simultaneously creating an inclusive and diverse school environment. Some of the main events we organize include: Semi formal, summer carnival, spirit week, and grad breakfast. We aim to make highschool the most memorable experience of your life!! Shoutout to the amazing teachers who supervise our meetings; we love you guys!
